*****************************************************
COMMANDS:
- Move: Mouse
- Restart: R
- Quit: Q

*****************************************************
INSTRUCTIONS and RULES:
- Avoid BALLS
- Collect SQUARES
- Every 5 points -> 1 BALL added (until 25 points)
- Every next BALL is bigger and faster
- BALLS always appear from the center
- After 25 points, nothing changes -> IT`s UP TO YOU
- 1 point = 1 % GAME COMPLETION
- 100 points / 100 % -> GAME WON
